Wordsearch Maker

Step 1: Add words by typing them in the add word text box and clicking the + button or pressing enter.

Step 2: Choose a grid size - larger grids will be harder to solve but less tightly packed with words.

Step 3: Choose the orientations you wish words to be placed with. At least one must be chosen.

Step 4: Click the Generate Word Search button to generate your word search.

You may save your current settings and reload them later, or reset to the default settings.
